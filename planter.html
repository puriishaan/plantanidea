The product

The Planter

A low-cost, AI-assisted seed planter that coats, scans, and sows — designed to be pushed by a single farmer and powered by the sun.

One machine, four breakthroughs.

From AI seed detection to solar-powered ease of use, every part of the planter is built to raise yields while lowering the barrier to good farming.

See it in action

Capabilities

🧠

AI

Our robust AI model customises spray rate by detecting seed type, and notifies farmers if any spoiled seeds have been planted.

🎯

Precision Farming

Farmers get accurate yield predictions and spatial maps of their plot, allowing them to predict their harvest.

💧

Germination Sprayer

Each seed is coated with the right amount of germination fluid as it's dispensed (traditionally cow urine).

☀️

Ease of Use

Attach a bottle of germination fluid, connect the device to a solar-charged portable battery, and push the machine.

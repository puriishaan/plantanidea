Plant An Idea

Plant An Idea's mission is to revolutionise farming through the development of a low-cost, efficient seed planter tailored to the needs of rural Indian farmers. By simplifying the sowing process, this innovative tool aims to increase productivity and crop yield, reduce manual labor, and promote sustainable agricultural practices. Through this initiative, we strive to bridge the technological gap and support farmers in building resilient livelihoods.
